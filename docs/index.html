What affects the projectile's penetration?

The folliwing values are for Metric Units only!

  • Ballistic Coefficient (Drag of the projectile), we will assign "c" to it.
  • Air density (Average density is ~1.2kg/m³), we will assign "ρ" to it.
  • Time in flight, we will assign "t" to it.
  • Mass of the projectile, we will assign "m" to it.
  • Muzzle velocity, we will assign "V0x" to it.
  • Height of discharge, we will assign "h" to it.
  • Gravity acceleration,(9.81 m/s²) we will assign "g" to it.
  • Cross section of the projectile, we will assign "A" to it.
  • velocity of the projectile, we will assign "V" to it.

Drag formula (to determine round's range):

x = V0x = -c p A v 2 t 2 2m

Example

v x = v cos θ = ( 10 m/s ) cos 30 = 8.66 m/s
v y = v sin θ = ( 10 m/s ) sin 30 = 5.00 m/s
